caturthaḥ paricchedaḥuktā vimalā buddhaguṇāḥ| tatkarma jinakriyedānīṃ vaktavyā| sā punaranābhogataścāpraśrabdhitaśca
samāsato dvābhyāmākarābhyā pravartata iti| anantaramanābhogāpraśrabdha
buddhakāryamārabhya dvau ślokau|vineyadhātau vinayābhyupāyevineyadhātorvinayakriyāyām|taddeśakāle
gamane ca nityaṃvibhoranābhogata eva vṛttiḥ

kṛtsnaṃ niṣpādya yānaṃ pravaraguṇagaṇajñānaratnasvagarbhaṃpuṇyajñānārkaraśmipravisutavipulānantamadhyāmbarābham|buddhatvaṃ
sarvasattve vimalaguṇanidhiṃ nirviśiṣṭaṃ vilokyakleśajñeyābhrajālaṃ
vidhamati karuṇā vāyubhūtā jinānām

etayoryathākramaṃ dvābhyāmaṣṭābhiśca ślokaiḥ piṇḍārtho veditavyaḥ|yasya yena ca yāvacca yadā
ca vinayakriyā|tadvikalpodayābhāvādanābhogaḥ sadā muneḥ

yasya dhātorvineyasya yenopāyena bhūriṇā|yā vinītikriyā yatra yadā taddeśakālayoḥ

niryāṇe tadupastambhe tatphale tatparigrahe|tadāvṛttau taducchittipratyaye cāvikalpataḥ

bhūmayo daśa niryāṇaṃ taddhetuḥ saṃbhṛtidvayam|tatphalaṃ paramā bodhirbodheḥ sattvaḥ parigrahaḥ

tadāvṛtiraparyantakleśopakleśavāsanāḥ|karuṇā tatsamudghātapratyayaḥ sārvakālikaḥ

sthānāni veditavyāni ṣaḍetāni yathākramam|mahodadhiravivyomanidhānāmbudavāyuvat

jñānāmbuguṇaratnatvādagrayānaṃ samudravat|sarvasattvopajīvyatvāt saṃbhāradvayamarkavat

vipulānantamadhyatvādbodhirākāśadhatuvat|samyaksaṃbuddhadharmatvāt sattvadhāturnidhānavat

āgantuvyāptyaniṣpa ttestatsaṃkleśo'bhrarāśivat|tatkṣiṃptipratyupasthānāt karuṇodvṛttavāyuvat

parādhikāraniryāṇāt sattvātmasamadarśanāt|kṛtyāparisamāpteśca kriyāpraśrabdhirā bhavāt

yadanutpādānirodhaprabhāvitaṃ buddhatvamityuktaṃ tatkathamihāsaṃskṛtādapravṛttilakṣaṇādbuddhatvādanābhogāpratipraśrabdhamā
lokādavikalpaṃ buddhakārya pravartata iti| buddhamāhātmyadharmatāmārabhya
vimatisaṃdehajātānāmacintyabuddhaviṣayādhimuktisaṃjananārtha tasya māhātmye
ślokaḥ|śakradundubhivan meghabrahmārkamaṇiratnavat|pratiśrutirivākāśapṛthivīvat tathāgataḥ

asya khalu sūtrasthānīyasya ślokasya yathākramaṃ pariśiṣṭena granthena vistaravibhāganirdeśo
veditavyaḥ|śakrapratibhāsatvāditi|viśuddhavaiḍūryamayaṃ yathedaṃ syānmahītalam|svacchatvāttatra
dṛśyeta devendraḥ sāpsarogaṇaḥ

prāsādo vaijayantaśca tadanye ca divaukasaḥ|tadvimānāni citrāṇi tāśca divyā vibhūtayaḥ

atha nārīnaragaṇā mahītalanivāsinaḥ|pratibhāsaṃ tamālokya praṇidhiṃ kuryurīdṛśam

adyaiva na cirādevaṃ bhavemastridaśeśvarāḥ|kuśalaṃ ca samādāya varteraṃstadavāptaye

pratibhāso'yamityevamavijñāyāpi te bhuvaḥ|cyutvā divyupapadyeraṃstena śuklena karmaṇā

pratibhāsaḥ sa cātyantamavikalpo nirīhakaḥ|evaṃ ca mahatārthena bhuvi syātpratyupasthitaḥ

tathā śraddhādivimale śraddhādiguṇabhāvite|sattvāḥ paśyanti saṃbuddhaṃ pratibhāsa svacetasi

lakṣaṇavyañjanopetaṃ vicitreryāpathakriyam|caṅkramyamāṇaṃ tiṣṭhantaṃ niṣaṇṇaṃ śayanasthitam

bhāṣamāṇaṃ śivaṃ dharma tūṣṇīṃbhūtaṃ samāhitam|citrāṇi prātihāryāṇi darśayantaṃ mahādyutim

taṃ ca dṛṣṭvābhiyujyante buddhatvāya spṛhānvitāḥ|taddhetuṃ ca samādāya prāpnuvantīpsitaṃ padam

pratibhāsaḥ sa cātyantamavikalpo nirīhakaḥ|evaṃ ca mahatārthena lokeṣu pratyupasthitaḥ

svacittapratibhāso'yamiti naivaṃ pṛthagjanāḥ|jānantyatha ca tatteṣāmavandhyaṃ bimbadarśanam

taddhi darśanamāgamya kramādasminnaye sthitāḥ|saddharmakāyaṃ madhyasthaṃ paśyanti jñānacakṣuṣā

bhūryadvatsyāt samantavyapagataviṣamasthānāntaramalāvaiḍūryaspaṣṭaśubhrā vimalamaṇiguṇā śrīmatsamatalā|śuddhatvāttatra
bimbaṃ surapatibhavanaṃ māhendramarutā-mutpadyeta krameṇa kṣitiguṇavigamādastaṃ
punariyāt

tadbhāvāyopavāsavrataniyamatayā dānādyabhimukhāḥpuṣpādīni kṣipeyuḥ praṇihitamanaso nārīnaragaṇāḥ|vaiḍūryasvacchabhute
manasi munipaticchāyādhigamanecitrāṇyutpādayanti pramuditamanasastadvajjinasutāḥ

yathaiva vaiḍūryamahītale śucausurendrakāyapratibimbasaṃbhavaḥ|tathā jagaccittamahītale śucaumunīndrakāyapratibimbasaṃbhavaḥ

bimbodayavyayamanāvilatāvilasva-cittapravartanavaṃśājjagati pravṛttam|lokeṣu yadvadavabhāsamupaiti
bimbaṃtadvanna tatsaditi nāsaditi prapaśyet

devadundubhivaditi|yathaiva divi devānāṃ pūrvaśuklānubhāvataḥ|yatnasthānamanorūpavikalparahitā
satī

anityaduḥkhānairātmyaśāntaśabdaiḥ pramādinaḥ|codayatyamarān sarvānasakṛddevadundubhiḥ

vyāpya buddhasvaraṇaivaṃ vibhurjagadaśeṣataḥ|dharma diśati bhavyebhyo yatnādirahito'pi san

devānāṃ divi divyadundubhiravo yaidvat svakarmodbhavodharmodāharaṇaṃ munerapi tathā loke svakarmodbhavam|yatnasthānaśarīracittarahitaḥ
śabdaḥ sa śāntyāvahoyadvat tadvadṛte catuṣṭayamayaṃ
dharmaḥ sa śāntyāvahaḥ

saṃgrāmakleśavṛttāvasurabalajayakrīḍāpraṇudanaṃdundubhyāḥ śabdahetuprabhavamabhayadaṃ yadvat
surapure|sattveṣu kleśaduḥkhapramathanaśamanaṃ mārgottamavidhaudhyānārūpyādihetuprabhavamapi
tathā loke nigaditam

ākāśavaditi|niṣkiṃcane nirābhāse nirālambe nirāśraye|cakṣuṣpathavyatikrānte'pyarūpiṇyanidarśane

yathā nimnonnataṃ vyomni dṛśyate na ca tattathā|buddheṣvapi tathā sarva dṛśyate na ca tattathā

pṛthivīvaditi|sarve mahīruhā yadvadavikalpāṃ vasuṃdharām|niśritya vṛddhiṃ vairūḍhiṃ vaipulyamupayānti
ca

saṃbuddhapṛthivīmevamavikalpāmaśeṣataḥ|jagatkuśalamūlāni vṛddhimāśritya yānti hi

udāharaṇānāṃ piṇḍārthaḥ|na prayatnamṛte keściddṛṣṭaḥ kurvan kriyāmataḥ|vineyasaṃśayacchittyai
navadhoktaṃ nidarśanam

sūtrasya tasya nāmnaiva dipitaṃ tatprayojanam|yatraite nava dṛṣṭāntā vistareṇa prakāśitāḥ

etacchratamayodārajñānālokādyalaṃkṛtāḥ|dhīmanto'vatarantyāśu sakalaṃ buddhagocaram

ityartha śakravaiḍūryapratibimbādyudāhṛtiḥ|navadhodāhṛtā tasmintatpiṇḍārtho'vadhāryate

darśanādeśanā vyāptirvikṛtirjñānaniḥsṛtiḥ|manovākkāyaguhyāni prāptiśca karuṇātmanām

sarvābhogaparispandapraśāntā nirvikalpikāḥ|dhiyo vimalavaiḍūryaśakrabimbodayādivat

pratijñābhogaśāntatvaṃ heturdhīnirvikalpatā|dṛṣṭāntaḥ śakrabimbādiḥ prakṛtārthasusiddhaye

ayaṃ ca prakṛto'trārtho navadhā darśanādikam|janmāntardhimṛte śāsturanābhogāt pravartate

etamevārthamadhikṛtyodāharaṇasaṃgrahe catvāraḥ ślokāḥ|yaḥ śakravaddundubhivat payodavadbrahmārkacintāmaṇirājaratnavat|pratiśrutivyomamahīvadā
bhavātparārthakṛdyatnamṛte sa yogavit

surendraratnapratibhāsadarśanaḥsudaiśiko dundubhivad vibho rutam|vibhurmahājñānakṛpābhramaṇḍalaḥspharatyanantaṃ
jagadā bhavāgrataḥ

anāsravādbrahmavadacyutaḥ padā-danekadhā darśanameti nirmitaiḥ|sadārkavajjñānaviniḥsṛtadyuti-rviśuddhacintāmaṇiratnamānasaḥ

pratirava iva ghoṣo'nakṣarokto jinānāṃgaganamiva śarīraṃ vyāpyarūpi dhruvaṃ ca|kṣitiriva nikhilānāṃ
śukladharmauṣadhīnāṃjagata iha samantādāspa daṃ buddhabhūmiḥ

kathaṃ punaranenodāharaṇanirdeśena satatamanutpannā aniruddhāśca buddhā bhagavanta utpadyamānā
nirudhyamānāśca saṃdṛśyante sarvajagati caiṣāmanābhogena buddhakāryāpratipraśrabdhieriti
paridīpitam|śubhaṃ vaiḍūryavaccitte buddhadarśanahetukam|tadviśuddhirasaṃhāryaśraddhendriyavirūḍhitā

śubhodayavyayāddhuddhaṃpratibimbodayavyayaḥ|munirnodeti na vyeti śakravaddharmakāyataḥ

ayatnāt kṛtyamityevaṃ darśanādi pravartate|dharmakāyādanutpādānirodhādā bhavasthiteḥ

ayameṣāṃ samāsārtha aupamyānāṃ kramaḥ punaḥ|pūrvakasyottareṇokto vaidharmyaparihārataḥ

buddhatvaṃ pratibimbābhaṃ tadvanna ca na ghoṣavat|devadundubhivat tadvanna ca no sarvathārthakṛt

mahāmeghopamaṃ tadvanna ca no sārthabījavat|mahābrahmopamaṃ tadvanna ca nātyantapācakam

sūryamaṇḍalavat tadvanna nātyanta tamo'paham|cintāmaṇinibhaṃ tadvanna ca no durlabhodayam

pratiśrutkopamaṃ tadvanna ca pratyayasaṃbhavam|ākāśasadṛśaṃ tadvanna ca śuklāspadaṃ ca tat

pṛthivīmaṇḍalaprakhyaṃ tatpratiṣṭhāśrayatvataḥ|laukyalokottarāśeṣajagatkuśalasaṃpadam

buddhānāṃ bodhimāgamya lokottarapathodayāt|śuklakarmapathadhyānāpramāṇārūpyasaṃbhava iti

iti ratnagotravibhāge mahāyānottaratantraśāstre tathāgatakṛtyakriyādhikāraścaturthaḥ paricchedaḥ
ślokārthasaṃgrahavyākhyānataḥ samāptaḥ
