ityukto bhagavatā| tadviśuddhirāśrayaparivṛtteḥ svabhāvo veditavyaḥ|

yaduktamāśrayaparivṛtteḥ svabhāvo viśuddhiriti tatra viśuddhiḥ samāsato dvividhā| prakṛtiviśuddhirvaimalyaviśuddhiśca|
tatra prakṛtiviśuddhiryā vimuktirna ca visaṃyogaḥ prabhāsvarāyāścittaprakṛterāgantukamalāvisaṃyogāt|
vaimalyaviśuddhirvimuktirvisaṃyogaśca vāryādīnāmiva

tatkarma svaparārthasaṃpādanamityuktam| tatra katamā svaparārthasaṃpat|

anāsravatvaṃ kleśānāṃ savāsanani rodhataḥ|asaṅgāpratighātatvājjñānasya vyāpitā matā

asaṃskṛtatvamatyantamavināśasvabhāvataḥ|avināśitvamuddeśastannirdeśo dhruvādibhiḥ

tadabhāvāddhruvaṃ jñeyaṃ śivaṃ śāśvatamacyutam|padaṃ tadamalajñānaṃ śukladharmāspadatvataḥ

yathānimittamākāśaṃ nimittaṃ rūpadarśane|śabdagandharaspṛśyadharmāṇāṃ ca śravādiṣu

indriyārtheṣu dhīrāṇāmanāsravaguṇodaye|hetuḥ kāyadvayaṃ tadvadanāvaraṇayogataḥ

yaduktamākāśalakṣaṇo buddha iti tatpāramārthikamāveṇikaṃ tathāgatānāṃ buddhalakṣaṇamabhisaṃdhāyoktam|
evaṃ hyāha| saṃceddvātriṃśanmahāpuruṣalakṣaṇaistathāgato draṣṭavyo'bhaviṣyattadrājāpi
cakravartī tathāgato'bhaviṣyaditi| tatra paramārthalakṣaṇe yogārthamārabhya ślokaḥ|acintyaṃ
nityaṃ ca dhruvamatha śivaṃ śāśvatamathapraśāntaṃ ca vyāpi vyapagatavikalpaṃ gaganavat|asaktaṃ
sarvatrāparatighaparuṣasparśavigataṃna dṛśyaṃ na grāhyaṃ śubhamapi ca buddhatvamamalam

atha khalvasya ślokasyārthaḥ samāsato'ṣṭābhiḥ ślokairveditavyaḥ|vimuktidharmakāyābhyāṃ svaparārtho

śrutasyāviṣayaḥ saukṣmyāccintāyāḥ paramārthataḥ|laukyādibhāvanāyāśca dharmatāgavharatvataḥ

dṛṣṭapūrvaṃ na tadyasmādvālairjātyandhakāyavat|āryaiśca sūtikāmadhyasthita bālārkabimbavat

utpādavigamānnityaṃ nirodhavigamāddhruvam|śivametaddvayābhāvācchāśvataṃ dharmatāsthiteḥ

śāntaṃ nirodhasatyatvādvyāpi sarvāvabodhataḥ|akalpamapratiṣṭhānādasaktaṃ kleśahānitaḥ

sarvatrāpratighaṃ sarvajñeyāvaraṇaśuddhitaḥ|paruṣasparśanirmuktaṃ mṛdukarmaṇyabhāvataḥ

adṛśyaṃ tadarūpitvādagrāhyamanimittataḥ|śubhaṃ prakṛtiśuddhatvādamalaṃ malahānitaḥ

yatpunaretadākāśavadasaṃskṛtaguṇāvinirbhāgavṛttyāpi tathāgatatvāmā bhavagateracintyamahopāyakruṇājñānaparikarmaviśeṣeṇa
